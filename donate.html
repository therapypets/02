More Ways to Support Us

AmazonSmile

×
AmazonSmile will donate .5% of your total purchase to the charity of your choice.

Just begin shopping on the AmazonSmile website and select Therapy Pet Pals of Texas on the pull down menu.

Give as you Get

×
Give as you Get gives you an easy way to buy the items you want with a percentage going to Therapy Pet Pals of Texas.

You'll continue to use your established shopping carts on some of the web's most popular stores. Now you can get what you want AND give to Therapy Pet Pals of Texas at the same time.

Giving Assistant

×
Giving Assistant lets you enjoy saving money on your online purchases while giving to Therapy Pet Pals of Texas. They also have an exclusive partnership with AmazonSmile that allows you to donate 2% per purchase!

Freytag's Florist

×
Freytag's Florist Austin Community Blooms program donates 5% of every sale to Therapy Pet Pals of Texas at no cost to you!

Simply mention or select "This is an Austin Community Blooms order" when placing an order online at www.freytagsflorist.com or by phone - (512) 345-4142.

Randall's Remarkable Card

×
Randall's Grocery will donate a percentage of your total shopping bill to Therapy Pet Pals of Texas when you designate your donation by linking our Good Neighbor Number - #2563 - to your Randalls' Remarkable Card.
