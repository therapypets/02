Benefits of Pet Therapy

Physical Therapy

Many patients will exercise their afflicted arms by reaching out and petting our therapy dogs / cats.

Mental Therapy

Withdrawn and non-communicative patients will often show interest in our therapy animals.

Validation Therapy

Pet Pals work as “ice-breakers” for TPPT volunteers to initiate conversations. The resident/patient will often talk about the pets they have known and loved.
